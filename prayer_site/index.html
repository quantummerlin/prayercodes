Your prayer is converted into a unique numerical code using English Gematria — an ancient practice where each letter has a numerical value (A=1, B=2, … Z=26). The total value of your prayer becomes your Prayer Code.

Your code becomes a concentrated form of your prayer intention — a spiritual shorthand you can carry, meditate on, and share throughout the day.

Your Privacy Matters

Prayer Codes is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how your information is handled.

Data Storage

All data — including your prayer journal, streaks, and prayer count — is stored locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage. No data is ever transmitted to any server. We have no database, no user accounts, and no way to access your prayers.

No Cookies

Prayer Codes does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics. Your browsing activity on this site is completely private.

Progressive Web App

If you install Prayer Codes as a PWA, it uses a service worker to cache assets for offline use. This is stored locally on your device and contains no personal information.

Third-Party Sharing

When you use the share feature, your prayer code is shared through your device's native sharing or social media links. We do not intercept, store, or log any shared content.
